Coffee shop sales

Tools Used: Tableau Data Extraction : Excel

Project Overview

A comprehensive analysis of a coffee shop chain's sales data to derive actionable business insights and optimize operations.

Dataset Description

    • Transaction details (ID, date, time, quantity)
    • Store information (ID, location)
    • Product metrics (ID, price, category, type, details)
    • Sales performance indicators

2. Data Analysis

  • Sales trend analysis
  • Product performance evaluation
  • Store-level comparisons
  • Time-based pattern recognition

Key Findings & Impact

There is a steady growth in sales from January to June,June recorded the highest revenue at $166.49K, showing an increasing trend.

  • More Revenue ii coming from coffee and least revenue coming from Packaged chocolate
  • Monday is the busiest day with $102k sales.,compared to weekends has marked lowest sales with an average of $97k
  • Most sold Item Flavours has less profitable compared to less sold Coffee Beans is more Profitable
  • Location-based insights
  • Revenue optimization opportunities

Business Recommendations

Top 5 Products by Sales are Coffee - $269.95K (highest revenue contributor),Tea - $196.41K,Bakery - $82.32K,Drinking Chocolate - $72.42K,Coffee Beans - $40.09K.

br> These products are the key revenue drivers, with coffee and tea contributing significantly.Bottom 5 Products by Sales Branded Items - $13.61K,Loose Tea - $11.21K,Flavours - $8.41K,Packaged Chocolate - $4.41K (lowest performer).These products have the lowest sales and might need promotional efforts or reconsideration.

  • The top five revenue-generating products are: Coffee - $269.95K (leading contributor) Tea - $196.41K Bakery - $82.32K Drinking Chocolate - $72.42K Coffee Beans - $40.09K These items significantly drive sales, with coffee and tea being the standout performers. Conversely, the bottom five products by sales are: Branded Items - $13.61K Loose Tea - $11.21K Flavours - $8.41K Packaged Chocolate - $4.41K (lowest performer) These low-performing items may benefit from targeted promotions or strategic adjustments to improve their sales performance.
